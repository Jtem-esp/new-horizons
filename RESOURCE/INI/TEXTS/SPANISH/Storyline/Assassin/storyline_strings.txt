As a former Dutch soldier, Johan Elting is regarded by his peers as a good fighter. Tired of the army life (and pay), he decided to become a smuggler. Little does he know that soon he will become one of the most feared mercenaries in the Spanish service. But how long will he accept this new status?{En su calidad de antiguo soldado holandés, Johan Elting es considerado por sus iguales como un buen luchador.
